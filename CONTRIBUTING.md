# Contributing to AIDEX

Thank you for your interest in the AI Development Excellence Standard (AIDEX). We are building the future of transparent and responsible AI engineering, and we need your help to make this standard robust and universal.

## Propose Changes to the Manifesto

If you believe a new ethical or technical rule should be added to the standard, please open an RFC (Request for Comments) in the Issues section. Use the [RFC] prefix in your title. Explain the problem, the proposed rule, and how it improves AI accountability.
