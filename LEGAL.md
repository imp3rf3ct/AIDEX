1. **Scope of Certification**
The AIDEX certification (Silver or Gold) validates transparency, documentation processes, and human oversight frameworks. It is a certification of methodology, not a guarantee of software performance, security, or absence of bugs.

2. **No Technical Guarantee**
The AIDEX Auditor does not certify the functional correctness of the code. AIDEX shall not be held liable for any damages—including but not limited to financial loss, data breaches, or system failures—arising from the execution of AI-generated code, even if said code was part of a certified repository.

3. **Responsibility of the "Public Human-in-Loop"** (PHiL)
Under the AIDEX standard, the designated Human-in-Loop remains the sole entity responsible for the AI's behavior and its consequences. The certification confirms that a human is accountable; it does not shift that accountability to AIDEX or its auditors.

4. **Revocation**
AIDEX reserves the right to revoke certification if fraud, "Shadow AI," or misrepresentation of logs is detected post-audit.
